Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe

Spaghetti Bolognese is a classic Italian dish featuring a rich meat sauce served over pasta, making it perfect for dinner any day of the week.

Description

This rich and hearty Bolognese sauce is simmered to perfection, allowing the flavors to deepen. The dish pairs beautifully with spaghetti for a satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes

Steps

  1. In a pan, cook ground beef until browned.
  2. Add chopped onion and garlic, and saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Pour in crushed tomatoes and simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Serve sauce over cooked spaghetti and garnish with parmesan.
