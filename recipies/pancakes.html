Pancake Recipe

Pancakes are fluffy breakfast favorites, perfect with syrup, butter, or fresh fruit. This simple recipe makes a batch of soft, golden pancakes in no time.

Description

These pancakes are light, fluffy, and perfect for a weekend breakfast or brunch. Made from simple ingredients like flour, eggs, and milk, they are easy to prepare and can be topped with your favorite additions.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix flour and sugar.
  2. Add milk and egg, and whisk until smooth.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan and pour 1/4 cup of batter.
  4. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook until golden brown.
  5. Serve with syrup or desired toppings.
